Core Features

Character Personalization

Choose your 3D model and name. See your unique character come to life in the game world, just as you designed it.

Dynamic Inventory

Manage your items with a flexible inventory system. Drag, drop, and even place objects directly into the game world.

Physics-Driven World

Experience a more realistic world with integrated physics for objects and players, including gravity and collision detection.

Community & LAN Play

Host your own server and play with friends on a local network. You own the game, you make the rules.

Development Roadmap

We believe in transparent development. Here's a glimpse of what's coming next:

  • Phase 1: Enhanced Player Experience - Improving inventory management and character customization.
  • Phase 2 & 3: Realistic Physics Engine - Introducing gravity, collisions for objects, and player physics for deeper immersion.
  • Phase 4: Advanced Player Interactions - Implementing player-to-player collision management.
  • Phase 5: A Richer World - Adding NPCs, quests, and direct player-to-player trading.
  • Long-Term Vision: A world-building tool for non-developers and migration to a more robust database.

About The Project

World RPG started as a passion project to create a lightweight, self-hostable MMORPG experience. It's built on the idea that players should have true ownership of their game worlds. Whether you're an indie developer looking for a foundation, a teacher creating an interactive lesson, or a group of friends wanting a private LAN adventure, this project is for you.

We are open-source and community-driven. Your feedback and contributions can help shape the future of this ever-evolving universe.
